                        
                             WELCOME!
                        
    Thank you for trying out Lilidog. If you have any questions
    or concerns, please post in the Lilidog discussion forum:
    https://sourceforge.net/p/lilidog/discussion/

    The main goal of Lilidog is to provide a stable lightweight
    full featured environment in which to work or play.

    Explore through the Tint2 Panel, Menus, and File Manager to
    see if this distro is right for you. Reboot to install.
    
    We are always on the lookout for new ideas and concepts. If
    you enjoy Lilidog and would like to contribute toward its
    development, feel free to opine in the forums.
     
    Enjoy!
