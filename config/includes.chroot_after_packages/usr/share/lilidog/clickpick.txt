	
Single Color Wallpaper Maker
	
Select OK to activate the
color picker.

Click on any color to make 
a new solid color wallpaper.
	
Click Cancel or press Esc a 
couple of times to exit.

Your wallpaper will change
to your new selection.

Your old wallpaper is where
you left it.

Your new wallpaper will be 
stored as Plain.png in
~/Pictures/wallpapers

Rename to save.
