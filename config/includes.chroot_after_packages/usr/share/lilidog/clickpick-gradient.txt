	
Directional Gradient Wallpaper
	
click twice for the colors,
then twice for the angles
of direction.

Select OK to activate the
color picker.
	
Click Cancel or press Esc a 
few of times to exit.
	
Your wallpaper will change
to your new selection.

Your old wallpaper is where
you left it.

Your new wallpaper will be 
stored as Gradient.png in
~/Pictures/wallpapers

Rename to save.
