	
Gradient Wallpaper Maker
	
Select OK to activate the
color picker.

Click on any two colors to
make a gradient wallpaper.
	
Or press Esc a few times
to exit.
	
Your wallpaper will change
to your new selection.

Your old wallpaper is where
you left it.

Your new wallpaper will be 
stored as Gradient.png in
~/Pictures/wallpapers

Rename to save.
