	
Gradient 4-way Wallpaper Maker
	
This one can be a bit slow
to finish after selections.

Select OK to activate the
color picker.

Click on any four colors to
make a gradient wallpaper.
	
Click Cancel or press Esc a 
few of times to exit.
	
Your wallpaper will change
to your new selection.

Your old wallpaper is where
you left it.

Your new wallpaper will be 
stored as Gradient-4.png
in ~/Pictures/wallpapers

Rename to save.
